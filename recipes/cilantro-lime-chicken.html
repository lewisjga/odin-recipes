Cilantro Lime Chicken

Description

This easy recipe saves you work and money, but still produces an amazing end product.

The beauty of this recipe is that all the work is done by your crockpot. So, if you're looking for a low effort meal, this is a great solution, the chicken comes out perfectly juicy and works great served with something simple like rice, or as a taco filling.

Ingredients

  • 3 Chicken Breasts (Boneless and Skinless)
  • 15 oz Tomato Sauce
  • 1 Bunch of Fresh Cilantro (Chopped)
  • Juice of 1 Lime
  • 1 tbsp Chili Powder
  • 1 tbsp Cumin
  • Garlic Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Add your chicken to the pot
  2. Cover with your dry seasonings and cilantro, adding your lime juice afterwards
  3. Pour in your tomato sauce
  4. Let cook either on low for 6 to 8 hours, or on high for 3 to 4
  5. Once it's finished cooking, shred the chicken and serve
